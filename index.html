What is Sumstack?

Sumstack is an all‑in‑one financial platform for Nigerian small businesses. Connect your bank, automate bookkeeping, and run quotes, invoices, bills, cards, and reports from one dashboard.

Do I need accounting experience?

No. Sumstack classifies transactions automatically and explains what changed. You can override or add simple rules.

How do payments work?

Send invoices with payment links. Customers can pay online; we reconcile the transaction automatically.

Can my team use cards?

Yes. Issue virtual or physical expense cards, set spend limits, and attach receipts from mobile.
